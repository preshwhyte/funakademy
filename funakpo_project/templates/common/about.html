Mission & Vision

We envision being a leading force in the industry, driven by innovation, integrity, and inclusivity, creating a brighter financial future for individuals and businesses while maintaining a strong commitment to customer satisfaction and community development

Mission

We are working together to provide a stimulating and happy learning environment in which our team of highly trained staff provide quality teaching in well resource buildings to address the special needs of our pupils and students in partnership with parents and guardians. We want to create a school in which children:

  • Are happy and safe
  • Achieve behavior and standards we are proud of by following the ‘Funakpo Way’
  • Are independent in their learning
  • Promote respect for themselves, others and the environment
  • Develop enquiring minds and a spirit of curiosity

Vision

To provide a place of excellence where children can achieve full potentials in their academic, creative, personal, physical, moral and spiritual development

Belief & Goals

We envision being a leading force in the industry, driven by innovation, integrity, and inclusivity, creating a brighter financial future for individuals and businesses while maintaining a strong commitment to customer satisfaction and community development

Belief

We believe that all children are made with different cognitive, affective and psychomotor abilities. That child “A”s development can never be like child “B”s. We developed and improved on the principle of individual learning. We believe that a child must be assisted as an individual to fully attain his highest potential while taken into cognizance his natural development pace. During school hours, different approaches and methodologies of teaching are implemented in order to enable pupils and students with diverse learning abilities make connection between the world around them and the school curricula. At FUNAKPO ACADEMY, we recognize every individual child’s needs and effectively work with them to achieve positive results.

    School Structure

    Funakpo Academy is headed by the school administrator who oversees the short and long terms management of the school as well as directly responsible for strategic planning and policy making of the school. The administrator persistently seeks for ways to upgrade and improve the quality and academic competence of the teachers and non-teaching staff. The School head coordinates the day to day activities of the school, he ensures the content delivery of the curricula is constantly and consistently checked and periodically evaluated for optimized results.

    School Day

    FUNAKPO ACADEMY school day starts at 8:00am through 2:00pm (Nursery) and 3:00pm (Primary and Secondary) Monday – Thursday and 12:00pm (Nursery) 1:00pm (Primary and Secondary) for Friday.
